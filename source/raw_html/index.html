The Ritual of Return

A daily journal to remember, re-center, and rehumanize myself.

1. How I Feel Today (Name it to claim it)

2. What Pulled Me Off-Center (If anything)

3. What Brought Me Back (Or what I wish would)

4. My Values in Action

5. What I Want to Remember

6. One Person (Real or Imagined) I Carry With Me

This is my ritual. I return to myself with each breath.
