The Ritual of Return

A daily journal to remember, re-center, and rehumanize myself.

My Living Values

Emotional Integrity

I honor the truth of my feelings, even when they are inconvenient. I believe emotions are intelligent, not irrational.

Self-Remembrance

I return to myself daily, not because I’m lost—but because the world tries to make me forget who I am.

Mutual Respect

I do not force others to understand me, nor do I allow myself to be diminished. All real connection begins with respect.

Radical Curiosity

I let questions breathe. I do not rush to closure. I believe confusion is a form of clarity in motion.

Embodied Wisdom

I trust my body’s knowing. What I feel in my chest, gut, and breath is not noise—it’s guidance.

Boundaried Openness

I am open—but not porous. I listen deeply, but I do not betray myself to keep peace.

Creative Sovereignty

My ideas, words, and insights are mine to shape, not mine to surrender for approval or belonging.
